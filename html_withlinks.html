HE+ PACKAGE

Drop your fuel costs by 13% with the Mack HE+ Package.*

01

Advanced Aerodynamics

The aerodynamic flanges, adjustable trim tabs and sleek hood design combine to help the Anthem cut through wind to reduce drag.

02

Energy Recovery Technology (ERT)

ERT captures heat from the engine exhaust and converts it to torque, burning up to 9.5% less fuel in highway operations.*

03

Mack Predictive Cruise

When cruise control is engaged,this intelligent navigation feature memorizes GPS data of up to 4,500 hills to engage the ideal shift strategy for repeated routes.

04

Our automated manual transmission monitors speed, grade, load and other variables to identify ideal shift points to ensure the truck is always in the right gear.
